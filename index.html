About our band

Bring Me the Horizon (often abbreviated as BMTH) are a British rock band, formed in Sheffield in 2004. The group currently consists of lead vocalist Oliver Sykes, guitarist Lee Malia, bassist Matt Kean, drummer Matt Nicholls and keyboardist Jordan Fish. They are signed to RCA Records globally and Columbia Records exclusively in the United States.

Music

A selection of our most popular songs

Amen



The lyrics of the song AmEN! tell the story of hatred towards people who are considered to be enemies. The singer expresses his hope that his enemy will feel the torment of hell. The song also calls for punishing the wicked and sanctifying oneself.Published on June 1, 2023.


Lost



In verse 1, we can see how he (the main character in this song) feels lost, abandoned by his friends, addicted to drugs/narcotics, and even lost his favorite dog. In addition, he also realizes that his ego is not his friend. released in early 2023


King Slayer



The song features a Collaboration with BABYMETAL and depicts themes such as rebellion, resistance to authority, and facing obstacles. Kingslayer" can also be a message of resilience and courage in the face of obstacles and challenges.


sTraNgeRs



Released on Wednesday, July 6, 2022. The lyrics involve themes of depression and mental illness, but the overall message is one of unity and togetherness, painting a picture that an entire 'room full of strangers' can have the same struggles and insecurities.
